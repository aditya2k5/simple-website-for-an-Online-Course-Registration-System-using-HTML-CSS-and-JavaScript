About Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham

Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham is a prestigious multi-campus private deemed university in India, founded in 1994 by humanitarian leader Sri Mata Amritanandamayi Devi (Amma). Headquartered in Ettimadai, Coimbatore, it spans 10 campuses across six states with 19 schools offering over 300 programs in engineering, medicine, management, arts, sciences, and more. The university holds the highest NAAC A++ accreditation and ranks 7th among Indian universities in NIRF 2024. Serving over 30,000 students with 2,000+ faculty, Amrita combines academic excellence with social commitment through initiatives like Live-in-LabsÂ®, benefiting 1 million rural residents. Key campuses include Coimbatore (engineering hub), Amritapuri (Kerala), Kochi (medical sciences), Bengaluru, and newer sites in Amaravati, Faridabad, and Nagercoil. Renowned for research innovation and global partnerships, Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham embodies its motto of "Innovation with Compassion," making it an ideal institution for your Electrical & Electronics Engineering studies.

About Courses

23EEE203-Embbeded systems

This course covers the fundamentals of embedded systems design and programming, including microcontroller architecture, real-time operating systems, and hardware-software co-design principles. it explores the development of embedded applications in various domains such as automotive, consumer electronics, and industrial automation. weekly assignments and a final project will provide hands-on experience in building embedded systems. also focuses on practical applications and industry-relevant skills for embedded system development. oppercunities for embedded system development are also discussed.

23CSE305-Data Science

This course introduces data science concepts, including data manipulation, statistical analysis, machine learning algorithms, and data visualization techniques using tools like Python and R. It emphasizes practical applications in extracting insights from large datasets across different industries. aims to equip students with the skills needed for data-driven decision-making and predictive modeling. with hands-on projects and case studies to reinforce learning. full stack data science skills are also covered.

23ECE335-VLSI Designing

This course focuses on the principles and techniques of Very Large Scale Integration (VLSI) design, covering topics such as CMOS technology, digital circuit design, layout methodologies, and testing strategies. It provides hands-on experience with VLSI design tools and methodologies for creating integrated circuits used in modern electronic devices. The course also explores low-power design techniques and emerging trends in VLSI technology. practical design projects and simulations are included to enhance understanding. career opportunities in VLSI design and related fields are also discussed.

23CSE306- AI and ML

This course explores artificial intelligence and machine learning concepts, including supervised and unsupervised learning, neural networks, deep learning architectures, and their applications in various domains. It provides hands-on experience with popular AI/ML frameworks and tools. The course emphasizes practical implementations of AI/ML algorithms for real-world problems, including natural language processing, computer vision, and recommendation systems. students will work on projects to apply AI/ML techniques to datasets. career opportunities in AI and ML fields are also discussed.
